\textbf{Question 12.13.3.38}\\
A and B throw a pair of dice alternately. A wins the game if he gets a total of
6 and B wins if she gets a total of 7. It A starts the game, find the probability of
winning the game by A in third throw of the pair of dice.\\
\solution
Let $X =\{X_1,X_2\}$be a random variable represent number on dice.\\
X=\brak{X_1+X_2\leq 12}
%\begin{table}[H]
%\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|}

 %X &0 & Bag 1\\
 %&1 & Bag 2\\
%\hline
% Y &0 & White ball\\
% &1 & Black ball\\

\begin{align}
p\brak{X_1+X_1=6} &= \frac{5}{36}\\
p\brak{X_1+X_2\neq6} &=1-\frac{5}{36} = \frac{31}{36}\\
p\brak{X_1+X_2=7} &= \frac{6}{36}\\
p\brak{X_1+X_2\neq7} &= 1-\frac{6}{36} = \frac{30}{36}
\end{align}
For A to win in third throw
\begin{align}
P\brak{A} &= p\brak{X_1+X_2\neq6}p\brak{X_1+X_2\neq7}p\brak{X_1+X_2=6}\\
          &= \frac{31}{36}\times\frac{30}{36}\times\frac{5}{36}\\
          &= 0.09
\end{align}
